Paras Khadka is known as one of the best players Nepal
 has ever produced and is current captain of the national
 side. He has also played at the Under-15 and Under-17 levels.
 A middle-order batsman who also bowls medium pace, he represented
 Nepal in the Under-19 World Cups of 2004, 2006 and 2008 while also
 being a part of the senior side. In the Plate semi-final against 
South Africa in 2006, Khadka was responsible for taking his side through to the finals.
 He was also the highest wicket-taker for Nepal in that tournament.
